FAQ

What is a good free VPN for a PC?

Trick question! There is no good free VPN. Free VPN services are essentially unsafe, mostly because being free raises questions about their funding and their budgets for research and maintenance. In other words, a free VPN provider is never good. Read a deeper dive into the subject in our dedicated article on free and paid VPNs.

How do I share a VPN connection from Android to PC?

If you want to share a VPN connection from Android to PC, you need to:

Step 1: Make sure your Android has cellular data or is connected to Wi-Fi.

Step 2: Connect your Android device to a VPN.

Step 3: Then, go to your Network settings and find Hotspot & tethering.

Step 4: Here, you have two options:

  • Either create a Wi-Fi hotspot and connect your Windows device to it.
  • Or use a USB cable to tether the connection directly.

How do I change my VPN on Windows PC?

To switch between VPN connections on Windows, all you need to do is:

Step 1: Right-click on the Windows menu icon at the left-bottom corner of your screen and choose Settings.

Step 2: Go to Network & Internet.

Step 3: On the right side of the screen, scroll down to the VPN section and click it.

Step 4: Here, you can manually add/change your VPN connections.

Does Windows 10/Windows 11 have a built-in VPN?

Yes, Windows has a built-in VPN. However, it is less convenient and less functional than VPN software. It’s best used to connect to the network at your work. But if you want to have access to a variety of servers around the world, you still need to subscribe to a service that maintains those servers to stay secure. At that point, it’s easier to just use that service’s app.
